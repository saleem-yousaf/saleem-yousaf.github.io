Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 June 2026

The short version

This is a personal site. I'm Saleem Yousaf and I run it. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages and uses a small amount of analytics. I don't sell or share your data, I don't run ads, and I don't have a newsletter list.

Who is the controller

For the purposes of UK GDPR, Saleem Yousaf is the data controller for this site. You can contact me at info@cyberspartans.co.uk.

What data is collected

Three categories, all minimal:

  • Hosting access logs. GitHub Pages, the host, records standard server logs (IP address, request URL, user agent, timestamp) for security and abuse prevention. These are GitHub's logs, not mine, and are subject to GitHub's privacy statement.
  • Cookies. This site sets no cookies of its own. Embedded fonts from Google Fonts may set cookies as part of how Google operates that service.
  • Voluntary contact. If you email me, your email and message are in my mailbox. I keep correspondence as long as it is useful, then delete it.

What it is used for

Logs are used for security and to understand which pages are read. The data is not used for profiling, marketing, or training any AI model. I don't run a CRM and there is no database of visitors.

Third parties

The following third parties may receive data when you visit this site:

  • GitHub (hosting). Receives all standard request data.
  • Google Fonts. Receives a request for the font files referenced in the page. Google may log the request.
  • Cloudflare-style CDN behaviour may apply to font and script delivery.

No data is sent to advertising networks, analytics dashboards, or AI providers from this site.

External links

This site links to my other properties: breachforge.co.uk and cyberspartans.co.uk, plus my profiles on LinkedIn, GitHub, Medium, Hashnode, Dev.to, Blogger, and CodePen. When you click one, you leave this site and become subject to that destination's privacy policy.

Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to access, rectify, or erase any personal data I hold about you, the right to restrict or object to processing, and the right to data portability. Practically, the only personal data I'm likely to hold is email correspondence you started. To exercise any of these rights, email info@cyberspartans.co.uk.

You also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk) if you believe I'm not handling your data lawfully.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13 and I do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

If the policy changes, the "Last updated" date at the top will change. Material changes will also be noted in a short summary on this page.
